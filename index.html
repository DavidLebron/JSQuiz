Objective of the project

Do a quiz game that work with simple rules and i could play without any adds unless like quiz app you can find everywhere.

Practice my Javascript skills

The rules

  • Chose your quizz and score the maximum points.
  • You have 3 life. If you don't answer in 20 seconds or if your answer is wrong, you lose a life.
  • The faster you answer, the most points you get.

Good Luck
